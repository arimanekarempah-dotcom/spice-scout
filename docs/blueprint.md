# **App Name**: SpiceScout

## Core Features:

- Nearby Lead Discovery: Uses real-time GPS detection to fetch business listings within a 5km radius via the Google Places API.
- AI Prospecting Tool: A generative AI tool that evaluates business data and reviews to provide a sales confidence score and relevance reasoning for spice distribution.
- Lead Map Visualization: Visualizes all nearby potential buyers and distributors on an interactive map with one-tap business card access.
- Prospect Management (Firestore): Saves high-value leads and previous search sessions to a persistent personal watchlist using Firestore.
- Action-Ready Lead Cards: Displays streamlined business cards with quick-access buttons for direct calling, navigation, and map sharing.
- Search Filters & Radius Toggles: Advanced filtering for business types, opening status, and search distance increments from 1km to 10km.
- Data Export Utility: Allows sales representatives to export current search results into Excel format for offline reporting and CRM integration.

## Style Guidelines:

- A sophisticated dark mode palette featuring a Primary Saffron Gold (#EBA014) representing spice quality, against a Deep Burnt Earth background (#1F1B16).
- Accent color: Paprika Red (#E64A19) used sparingly for active lead indicators and critical CTA buttons to evoke energy and movement.
- Font pairing: 'Space Grotesk' for bold, technical headlines to feel modern and precise; 'Inter' for UI labels and data blocks for maximum readability on small mobile screens.
- Sharp, lightweight outline icons (Material Design 3 style) to maintain a professional sales-tool aesthetic without visual clutter.
- A bottom-heavy mobile navigation structure (Search, Map, Favorites, History) optimized for one-handed operation while in the field.
- Micro-interactions including pulse effects for GPS searching and smooth slide-up sheets for business details and filters.
